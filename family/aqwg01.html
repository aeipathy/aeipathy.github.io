Descendants of Johann Gottlieb WEICHE Sr.

First Generation

1. Johann Gottlieb WEICHE Sr. was born in 1797 in Mimeuritz, Brandenburg, West Prussia. He died on 17 Aug 1844 in Vandsburg, West Prussia from Falling tree.

Johann resided in Mimeuritz, Brandenburg, West Prussia. He resided from 1817/1827 to 1829 in New Strelitz. He resided in From 1829 to in Vandsburg, West Prussia. He worked as Tailor apprentice. He worked as Farmer. He worked as Musician with violin.

Johann married Dorothea Elisabeth HINZ in 1817 in probably in Trieste. Dorothea was born in 1800 in Trieste. She died in 1847 in Vandsburg, West Prussia.

Juliane Henriette WEICHE was born on 4 Jun 1827 in NeuStrelitz, Brandenburg, West Prussia. She was baptised on 8 Jun 1828 in Kolmar (Chodziesen), Posen, West Prussia. She died on 12 Nov 1920 in Medaryville, Indiana. She was buried in Medaryville, Pulaski Co., Indiana.

Juliane resided in NeuStrelitz, Brandenburg, West Prussia. She resided at a farm in 1829 in near Vandsburg, West Prussia. She resided from 1853 to 1858 in Michigan City, Indiana. She immigrated in 1853 to Michigan City, Indiana, USA. She resided from 1858 to 1880 in Wanatah, Indiana. She resided at a farm from 1880 to 1902 in 2.5 miles east of Knox (Indiana?). She resided at a farm owned by daughter and son-in-law, Lewis and Lena Werth, from 1902 to 1907 in 4 miles SW of North Judson in Rich Grove Township, Pulaski County, Indiana. She resided at with her daughter Mrs. A. I. Belger from 1907 to 1909 in Medaryville, Indiana. She resided at with her daughter Mrs. Werth from 1909 to 1913. She resided at with her daughter Matitda (Mrs. Belger) in 1913. Juliane attended Prussian Common School.
       
Juliane married August WARNER in 1846 in Vandsburg, West Prussia. August was born on 18 May 1821 in Posen, West Prussia. He died on 17 Feb 1907 in Pulaski Co., Indiana. He was buried in Medaryville, Pulaski Co., Indiana.

August was also known as August Werner.
