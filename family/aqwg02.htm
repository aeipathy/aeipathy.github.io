John was also known as John Gottlieb Weiche. He resided a short time in Michigan City. He emigrated in Apr 1853 from Bremen, Germany to the USA. He immigrated on 23 Aug 1853 to New York Harbor, USA from aboard The Brigantine SOPHIE. He resided at a farm in Spring 1863 in Wanatah, Indiana. He was a member of Christian? ("god-fearing"). He worked in Saw-mill, Michigan City, Indiana. He worked as Farmer.

Ricka was a member of Christian. She worked as sold wild huckleberries for 5 cents a quart.
